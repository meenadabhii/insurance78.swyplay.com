Allowance Management

Learn how to manage your allowance wisely by creating a simple budget plan.

Budgeting Tips for Teens:

  • Track your spending for a month
  • Divide money into categories: spending, saving, giving
  • Set financial goals (new phone, car, college)
  • Use budgeting apps designed for teens

Pro Tip: Try the 50/30/20 rule - 50% for needs, 30% for wants, and 20% for savings.

Saving for Goals

Whether it's a new video game, car, or college, we'll help you create a savings plan.

Smart Saving Strategies:

  • Set specific, measurable goals
  • Automate savings if possible
  • Look for ways to increase income (part-time job, chores)
  • Avoid impulse purchases - practice delayed gratification

College Preparation

Start planning for your future education now with our college savings guide.

Ways to Save for College:

  • 529 Plans - Tax-advantaged education savings accounts
  • Coverdell ESA - Education savings account for K-12 and college
  • UTMA/UGMA Accounts - Custodial accounts for minors
  • Scholarships and grants - Free money for college

Did You Know? Starting to save early can reduce the amount of student loans you'll need later.
